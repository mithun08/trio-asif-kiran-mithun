Real data is messy

Corrupt PDFs, a missing spreadsheet column, a scanned-image CV, a start date in the past. Any of these could crash a naive pipeline mid-run.

"Is it actually good?"

Up to now, quality is anecdotal ("Aarav looks right"). A product needs a measured pass rate against known-correct answers.
