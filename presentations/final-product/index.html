Product OverviewThe whole picture

The problemA growing firm, staffed by hand in a spreadsheet

Consultants are always in motion — on the bench, rolling off, or joining. Open roles shift weekly. Today a manager reconciles the two by judgment, in a free-text spreadsheet column.

The firm grows ~5% headcount a month. The manual approach doesn't scale — and leaves no record of why anyone was chosen.

What it weighsSix dimensions, each earning its weight

DimensionWeightCaptures
Skill match35%technical & domain fit

Performance trend5%direction of travel

Output is bands — Strong / Partial / Gap — and a "N of 6 strong" headline. Never a fake-precise "76% match".

Every weight and threshold is configurable, so staffing leadership can tune the engine to the firm's priorities without touching code.

The scorecard5 / 8

Why you can trust itDesigned to be auditable, not a black box

Math sets the ranks

The AI never decides who wins. It only reads text and writes explanations — the order is deterministic, reviewable code.

Grounded, not guessed

Every claim in an explanation must cite the source text. Unsupported claims are dropped, not scored.

Privacy by design

Personal and client details are scrubbed before any text reaches an external model — and re-attached only locally.

Nothing hidden

No one is silently dropped. Filtered-out people appear with the reason, and unfillable roles get a constructive gap analysis.
